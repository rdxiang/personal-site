        In today's technology-filled world, video games have become an integral part of modern life. People of all ages play video games, and while it's obvious that video games have entertainment value, it's not always clear whether the benefits outweight the costs. In this website, we'll address both sides of the video gaming world, the good and the bad. It's up to you to decide whether video games are worth their weight.
