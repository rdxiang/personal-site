The Last Wish: Travel

To be able to travel the entire world and experience a diversity of cultures.

The Backstory

After wishing for high quality education and a sustainable environment for the world, it would be a nice treat to be able to see the changes for ourselves. Of course, the only way we could possibly do that is with the help of a little genie magic. To visit all 196 countries in the world and truly immerse ourselves in so many different cultures would take an awfully long time, more time than any one person has. Not to mention, it would definitely run the average bank account dry sooner or later. So, our final wish is to experience the world for all it is, since we only have one.
